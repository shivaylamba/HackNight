About

Skin Scanner is machine learning applied to healthcare. It is an image processing and classification algorithm for skin conditions.
The idea is simple: submit a picture of the affected area of your skin and our algorithm will scan it for visual match with one of the major diseases.

Take a picture

Take a picture of the affected area of your skin with a digital camera or smartphone app. Try to keep focus on the area and avoid blurring.

Upload

Upload the picture in jpg pgn jpeg or gif format via the SkinScanner home page

Classify

The algorithm runs a trained deep learning visual recognition model on your picture, outputting the type and likelihood of any of the 23 top-level skin diseases
